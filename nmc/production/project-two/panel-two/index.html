Hospital Playlist

Hospital Playlist, also known as "Wise Doctor Life", depicts the stories of doctors, nurses and patients at a hospital. 5 doctors all entered the same medical university in 1999. They are now friends and work together in the same hospital.

  • Jo Jung-Seok as Lee Ik-Joon
  • Yoo Yeon-Seok as Ahn Jung-Won
  • Jung Kyung-Ho as Kim Joon-Wan
  • Kim Dae-Myung as Yang Seok-Hyung
  • Jeon Mi-Do as Chae Song-Hwa

Along with the Gods: The Two Worlds

Following a person's a death, an Angel of Death escorts the deceased to the afterworld. There, the deceased person has 7 trials over a period of 49 days. At that time, the Angel of Death, who are not supposed to get involved in human affairs, unavoidably take part in human affairs.

  • Ha Jung-W00 as Gang-Rim
  • Cha Tae-Hyun as Kim Ja-Hong
  • Joo Ji-Hoon as Hae Won-Mak
  • Kim Hyang-Gi as Lee Deok-Choon
