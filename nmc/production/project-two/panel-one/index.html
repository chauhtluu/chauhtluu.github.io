Hop On Over

Beauty Inside

A 2015 South Korean romantic comedy film based on the 2012 American social film The Beauty Inside, about a man who wakes up every day in a different body.

Psychopath's Diary

The story of a man who coincidentally witnesses a murder and picks up the killer's diary. While fleeing the scene, he gets into a car accident causes him to lose his memory and mistakenly believes that he is a psychopathic serial killer.

Joy of Life

A normal civilian's life is suddenly disrupted by assassins, politics, and his secret background. He learns martial arts, and finds himself in an arranged marriage with Lin Wan'er, a chicken drumstick princess. Recommended by my friend, Maria.

Doctor's Playlist

"Wise Doctor Life" depicts the stories of doctors, nurses and patients at a hospital. 5 doctors all entered the same medical university in 1999. They are now friends and work together in the same hospital.
